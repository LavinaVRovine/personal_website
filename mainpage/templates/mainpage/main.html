ABOUT ME

While getting my master's degree in Library and Information science, I've found liking in spreadsheets. For unknown reasons, I found joy in putting data into tables, writing formulas to analyze them and subsequently plotting them, with hopes of getting insights of various issues.

Since then I've became experienced data analyst working with various tools, which enables me to dig deep into the data. I prefer thorough over shallow, quality over quantity, and as such, I like to get hands dirty (metaphorically) and make the data speak.

In recent years, I've started gravitating more and more towards data science, as it's the field, where I can combine my love for data analysis with passion for programming.
